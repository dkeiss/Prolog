==============================
===== List examples =====
==============================

Concat lists:
X=[b,c],L=[a|X].
X=[c,d],L=[a,b|X].

Head and rest:
[[a,b],[c,d],[e,f]]=[H|Re].
[[a,b],[c,d],[e,f]]=[H1,H2|Re].
[A|[B,C,D]] = [1,2,3,4].

Stackoperations:
Push: S=name('hans'),NS=[name('Klaus')|S].
Pop: S=[a,b,c,d], S=[_|NS].
Top of the Stack: S=[a,b,c,d], S=[ToS|_].
